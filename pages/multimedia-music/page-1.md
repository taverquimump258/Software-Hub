## Programs

### [Pandora One](https://hackmd.io/-tuTaQgxSpa8KlVGH41r8g)
Pandora One is a subscription-based music streaming service that offers ad-free listening, unlimited skips, and higher-quality audio. It allows users to create custom radio stations based on their music preferences.

### [Crescendo Masters](https://hackmd.io/P9JxIRn3TPSL_4gbYWD-0g)
Crescendo Masters is a music notation software that allows users to create, edit, and print sheet music. It offers tools for composing, arranging, and scoring music with support for multiple instruments and playback features.
